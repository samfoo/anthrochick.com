Books & Craft

Case Study

Overview

Books & Craft creates personalised stationary to order. This small business was transitioning from being a side hobby with friends requesting orders to something much bigger. As the transition grew the Client was facing greater difficulties tracking orders

My Role

  • Lead UX Consultant
  • Identify business needs
  • Ideate for a solution
  • Manage stakeholder expectation
  • Product testing for learnibility

Project Duration

1 week project

Tools

  • Stakeholder Interviews
  • Sketching
  • Wireframing
  • User Testing
  • POP- Prototype on Paper- interactive prototype app

Approach

Building trust by focusing on the client

By empathising with the stakeholders situation and keeping the informed of the direction we are taking. Understanding that new directors are often protective of their budding business so it's important to to keep them involved and any work should be phased in the 'we' tense to help encourage feelings of collaboration and transperency. It's very important for our client that we are making their job easier so we need to make sure our research is through and they trust us with the information they are providing.

Methods

In this scenario I relied heavily on sketching materials and user flows. I spoke to my client daily about the strategy and the steps I was taking to impliment a solution. I decided to design for a mobile device. I performed user testing. In the final stages and I created an interactive POP application.

Previous Client Scenario

  1. Client is a small business owner
  2. She travels light, relying on her memory when taking orders in the field.
  3. She often forgets the details of orders and has to reconfirm them.

Researched the stages the client moves through when taking an order and created a flow of the stages that the client realies on for her business.

Introducing the Clients to Useful Tools

In client interview it was revealed that the client rarely carries anything other than their phone. Our solution was to create a mobile app for internal order traking, while also offering the client a way to show examples of existing work as they discusses B&C's products.

Techniques

  • Obersvational and discussion based.
  • Learnibility testing on 8 participants
  • Prototyping, iteration and redesign.

Design Features

  • Large buttons
  • Opportunies to go back.
  • Inclusing of all aspects of the order.
  • Login on when necessary to secure client information, therefore not interrupting the New Order Process.
  • Will impliment the Clients existing branding and images.
  • Allows fast access to gallery to showcase previous work.
  • Allows for fast progress check on each product.

New Client Scenario

Now the client has a tool always on them

Client now uses tool to remember order details and complete their work.
