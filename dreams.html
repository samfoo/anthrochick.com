Parrot Sanctuary

I used to work with parrot rehabilitation. I aim to get back into it once I can secure a large avery. Parrots are wonderful creatures and people often want to keep them as pets. As parrots live for around 100 years, parrots often outlive their owners. In these cases, birds are typically abandoned. I want to create an avery charity to house abandoned birds.
