What is anthropology & how does it relate to UX?

Anthropology is a discipline that greedily seeks knowledge of human behaviour and everything humans are involved with. UX is obsessed with the xperiences that a person has with a product or a service.

Anthropology focuses on research and observation. It involves being immersed in the experiences of others, what their motivations are, and which belief systems they ascribe to. UX is a process of designing to the cultural conditioning that a given human ascribes to. Together, UX and anthropology, are powerful tools that allow you to understand why someone does what they do and how to optimise for those behaviours.

In Short...

Anthropology + Design = Human Centered Design
