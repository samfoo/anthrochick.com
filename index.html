Transport for NSW

Experience design for transit passengers

Transport for NSW is responsible for improving the customer experience, planning, program administration, and regulation for all transport services within the Sydney metropolitan area.

Transport for NSW is collaborating with the city of Sydney to encourage and support the usage of public transport during off-peak hours. They approached us to find ways of improving the rider experience and to turn non-riders into riders. The deadline for the program is in 2030, so there's lots of time to experiment.
